Services Ever

Indonesia, a global powerhouse in coconut production, stands tall as an unrivaled source of premium coconuts sought after by companies and individuals worldwide. Renowned for its exceptional quality, Indonesian coconuts have earned global recognition, captivating the discerning tastes of customers across the globe. With its bountiful supply of top-notch coconuts, Indonesia continues to reign as a trusted provider, satisfying the growing demand for the finest coconut products on a global scale.

What We Do?

Driven by our commitment to sustainability and fair trade practices, we work closely with local coconut farmers, fostering long-term partnerships that ensure ethical sourcing and support the livelihoods of farming communities. Our dedication to social responsibility and environmental stewardship is ingrained in every aspect of our operations.

OurVission

To be the leading supplier of coconut derivative products in Indonesia, renowned for our competence, integrity, and innovative approach in delivering coconut derivative products that excel on the global stage.
